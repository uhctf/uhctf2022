Password policy

Your password must adhere to the following to ensure complexity:

  • not start with a letter
  • not end with a year
  • contain 3 special characters (!$^*@)
  • contain 2 digits, all in the second half
  • contain no letters from your username
  • contain no whitespace
  • consecutive characters are of different categories
  • be 8 characters long
